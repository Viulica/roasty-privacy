Roasty Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 Feb 2026

Roasty respects your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address so we can create and manage your account.
  • App data: Content you create in the app—such as calendar events, notes, and to-dos—is stored so we can provide sync and features.

How We Use This Information

We use your data to:

  • Create and secure your account
  • Store and sync your events, notes, and to-dos
  • Provide app features (e.g. summaries and reminders)
  • Respond to support requests

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for third-party advertising.

Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Firebase (Google) for authentication and cloud storage. Your account and app data are stored and processed by Firebase in accordance with Google's privacy policy.
  • Apple for Sign in with Apple and in-app subscriptions. Payment is handled by Apple; we do not receive or store your payment details.

Data Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your data in line with our retention practices and applicable law.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete or correct your data inside the app where those options are available
  • Request deletion of your account and data by contacting us at dermit.stela@gmail.com
  • Use your device and Apple/Google account settings to manage sign-in and data sharing

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data: dermit.stela@gmail.com
