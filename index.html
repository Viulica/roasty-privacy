Roasty

Your schedule, notes, and to-dos — all in one place.

What is Roasty?

Roasty is a voice-powered productivity app that helps you manage your schedule, tasks, and reminders. Tap the mic, speak your plans, and Roasty organizes them into calendar events and to-do items automatically. Roasty also sends you smart reminders with a twist — it holds you accountable with personalized roast-style notifications that you can set to light, mild, or savage.

How Roasty Works

Roasty lets you add events and tasks using your voice. It syncs with your existing Google Calendar so your schedule stays in one place. Before each event, Roasty sends you a heads-up reminder. After events, it checks in to make sure you followed through. You can also manage your weekly schedule, create notes, and organize tasks — all from one app.

How Roasty Uses Your Google Data

Roasty requests access to your Google Calendar to provide its core functionality. Specifically, Roasty uses this access to:

  • Read your existing calendar events so it can display your schedule within the app
  • Create new calendar events based on your voice input
  • Send you timely reminders before and after your scheduled events

Roasty does not sell, share, or transfer your Google data to any third parties. Your data is only used to provide and improve the features described above. Roasty's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Your Privacy

Your data is yours. Roasty only accesses the minimum Google Calendar data needed to deliver the features described above. You can revoke Roasty's access to your Google account at any time through your Google Account permissions.
