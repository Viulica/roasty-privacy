Roasty Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 Feb 2026

Roasty ("we," "our," or "the app") respects your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and with whom we share it.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive your name and email address so we can create and manage your account.
  • Google Calendar data: If you grant access, we read, create, update, and delete calendar events on your behalf using the Google Calendar API. This data is used solely to provide the app's calendar features.
  • App data: Content you create in the app—such as calendar events, notes, and to-dos—is stored so we can provide sync and features.

How We Use This Information

We use your data to:

  • Create and secure your account
  • Read, create, update, and delete calendar events at your direction
  • Store and sync your events, notes, and to-dos
  • Provide app features (e.g. summaries and reminders)
  • Respond to support requests

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for third-party advertising. We do not use Google user data for any purpose other than providing or improving the app's user-facing features.

Sharing, Transfer, and Disclosure of Google User Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your Google user data to any third parties. We do not share, transfer, or disclose your Google user data to third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Firebase (Google): Your account information and app data are processed and stored by Firebase, which is part of Google Cloud. This is necessary to operate the app's core functionality (authentication and cloud storage). Firebase processes your data in accordance with Google's privacy policy.
  • Apple: If you use Sign in with Apple or in-app subscriptions, Apple processes your authentication and payment data. We do not receive or store your payment details.

We do not transfer or disclose your Google user data to any other parties for advertising, data brokering, reselling, credit assessment, lending, or any purpose unrelated to providing the app's functionality.

Data Protection

We take the security of your data seriously and implement the following measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS.
  • Data stored in Firebase is encrypted at rest using Google Cloud's built-in encryption.
  • Access to user data is restricted to the minimum necessary to operate and maintain the app.
  • We regularly review our security practices to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your data.

Data Retention

We keep your data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your data in line with our retention practices and applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete or correct your data inside the app where those options are available
  • Request deletion of your account and data by contacting us at dermit.stela@gmail.com
  • Use your device and Apple/Google account settings to manage sign-in and data sharing
  • Revoke the app's access to your Google account at any time via your Google Account permissions

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
